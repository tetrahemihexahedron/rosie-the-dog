About this Website

As well as being a showcase for the exploits of Rosie the Dog, this website is a playground for Sunny Fawcett to practice modern CSS. While it hasn’t always been the case and likely won’t remain so in the future, right now the site uses only vanilla HTML and CSS.

Since the goal is to learn and gain experience, her use of AI has been limited to accepting small carefully reviewed and frequently edited tab completions in Cursor and occasionally using ChatGPT and Cursor’s Plan mode as virtual rubber ducks when evaluating different approaches. Her experience is that writing well-organized, easily maintained and extended CSS is not a strength of AI. She hopes to become a more effective guide for AI agents, and human teammates, by gaining more hands-on experience with modern CSS features.

For resources, she’s watched quite a few YouTube videos and occasionally discussed CSS in the park, and now she is now taking Josh Comeau’s CSS for JavaScript Developers course.
